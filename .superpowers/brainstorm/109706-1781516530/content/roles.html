Who does what?

Permissions by role

A

Identical Access

Contractors and managers see & do exactly the same things with assets. No restrictions.

B

Managers Have Extra Controls

Both can create/edit/retire assets. But only managers can: delete permanently, transfer ownership, view audit trails.

C

Contractors Need Approval

Contractors can suggest edits or retirements, but a manager must approve before changes take effect.

D

Something Else

Different permission model than these
