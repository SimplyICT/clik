How does an asset get created?

Who creates it and where?

A

Admin Creates, Contractor Deploys

Admin enters asset details, generates QR code. Contractor receives it, installs on-site, scans to activate.

B

Contractor Creates On-Site

Contractor installs equipment, creates asset record from their phone. Admin reviews and approves later.

C

Either / Both Paths

Admin can pre-create. Contractor can create in the field. Both generate QR codes. Synced via the same system.
