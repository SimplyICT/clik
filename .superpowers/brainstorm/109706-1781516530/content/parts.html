Parts & Components

How does this work?

A

Tracked Sub-Assets

Each part is its own asset record with a parent_id linking it to the main asset. Has its own QR code, service history, and lifecycle.

B

Inventory / Consumables

Parts are inventory items (filters, belts, bulbs). Not individually tracked. Contractor records "used 2 filters" during a job.

C

Both

Major components are tracked as sub-assets. Consumables are inventory items. Different treatment for each.
