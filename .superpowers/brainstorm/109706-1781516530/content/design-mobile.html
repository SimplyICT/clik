Design: Mobile PWA — Asset Views

New tab: "Assets" in contractor dashboard

Alongside existing "Jobs" tab. Shows assets assigned to this contractor or at their current location.

Screens:

ScreenWhat it shows
Asset ListFilterable list of assets (by category, status). QR scan button in header.
Asset DetailAll fields, photos, parts list, service history. Buttons: Create Job, Edit, Retire.
Create AssetForm with all fields + photo capture from camera. QR auto-generated.
QR ScannerCamera-based scan → opens asset detail. Uses device camera via PWA.
Create JobSelect job type (install/move/retire/inspect/repair/transfer), auto-links to asset ID, creates request.
Record PartsDuring a job: select parts used, enter quantities, submit.
